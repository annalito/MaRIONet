Launch Meeting in London

The manycore network launch meeting was at Imperial College London room 144 on Wednesday 11 January 2017. At this initial event we assessed the current state of manycore research and planned our coordinated actions for the next few years.

Meeting Agenda

The schedule for the day is published below. We had three keynote speakers, along with a range of lightning talks and networking activities. We regret that MaRIONet funding does not pay for your travel expenses.

The launch event was in room 144 of the Huxley Building at Imperial College London, South Kensington Campus.

June 2019: Second UK OpenMP Users Conference

Host a Manycore Event

If you want to organise a manycore event in the UK, we can help with funding and publicity. Please get in touch with us by visiting our contact page.

Please provide some information about your event plans. The network can provide limited funding for venue hire, catering costs and speaker travel expenses.

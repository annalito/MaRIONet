Upcoming Events

June 2018: Adaptive Manycore Systems Workshop

Adaptive Many-Core Architectures and Systems is an informal workshop which will be held in York from the 13th to the 15th of June 2018. The workshop aims to highlight and discuss emerging trends and future directions in the field of manycore system design (and beyond), and will feature invited position papers from world-leading researchers and industrialists across the field.

July 2018: Manycore Summer School 2018

The Manycore Summer School runs Mon 16 to Fri 20 July at the University of Glasgow. We have seven confirmed speakers from Arm, Oracle, Princeton, and elsewhere. We are now accepting applications for attendance from UK-based PhD students and postdocs. The summer school has free registration, including accommodation --- thanks to generous funding from EPSRC and SICSA.

September 2018: POETS Workshop in Southampton

POETS Workshop on Event-Based Parallel Computing provides a forum for engineers and researchers in academia, industry and government to present their latest results or work in progress in all aspects of parallel and distributed computing. The Workshop concerns all aspects of the research, development, and application of high-performance parallel-computing, specifically algorithms, applications, architecture, interprocessor communication, software, and performance.

Host a Manycore Event

If you want to organise a manycore event in the UK, we can help with funding and publicity. Please get in touch with us by visiting our contact page.

Please provide some information about your event plans. The network can provide limited funding for venue hire, catering costs and speaker travel expenses.
